Our Services

Examining essential services

InCheck 360 centralizes operational workflows, enabling F&B and retail brands to standardize performance, streamline daily tasks, and gain actionable visibility across locations.

Operations task management

Digitize opening, closing, and food safety routines with guided checklists, clear ownership, and timestamps across every shift and store.

Deployment & reference materials

Distribute SOPs, brand standards, and playbooks by role or location so teams always work from the latest version—no outdated binders.

Workflow automation

Trigger reminders, escalations, and follow-ups based on task status, missed checks, or non-compliance events in real time.

Operational visibility

Track completion rates, violations, and recurring issues through real-time dashboards that surface where attention is needed.

Project & rollout tracking

Coordinate LTOs, campaigns, and equipment rollouts with clear milestones and store-level execution tracking.

Escalations & communications

Route incidents and critical issues to the right managers instantly, with full history, comments, and resolution tracking.
