Our Services

Examining Essential Services

InCheck 360 centralizes operational workflows so multi-unit F&B and retail brands can standardize performance, streamline daily tasks, and gain real-time visibility across every store.

Operations Task Management

Turn opening, closing, and food safety routines into guided digital checklists with clear ownership, timestamps, and follow-ups for every shift.

Deployment & Reference Materials

Distribute SOPs, brand standards, and playbooks by role, shift, or region. Teams always see the latest version—no more outdated PDFs or binders.

Workflow Automation

Trigger reminders, escalations, and corrective actions automatically based on task status, overdue items, or incomplete checklists.

Operational Visibility

Dashboards surface completion rates, compliance scores, and repeat issues so supervisors know exactly where to focus.

Project & Rollout Tracking

Coordinate LTOs, equipment installations, and new initiatives with rollout timelines and store-level tracking.

Escalations & Communications

Route incidents and critical issues to the right manager instantly, with full context and audit trails.
